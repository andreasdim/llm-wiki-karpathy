Outcome that allows scoping the internal volume zones created in an operation, making them available for subsequent operations.

## Overview

**Internal Volume Zone Scope** lets you scope the internal volume zones created by an operation. Add it by right-clicking a **Step** and selecting **Insert > Internal Volume Zone Scope**.

## Steps That Produce This Outcome

- Steps that create internal volume zones; added manually via **Insert > Internal Volume Zone Scope**
