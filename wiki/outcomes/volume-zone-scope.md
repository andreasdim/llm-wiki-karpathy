Outcome that allows scoping created volume zones in an operation, making them available for subsequent operations.

## Overview

**Volume Zone Scope** lets you scope the volume zones created by an operation. Add it by right-clicking a **Step** and selecting **Insert > Volume Zone Scope**.

## Details View

### General

| Field | Description |
|---|---|

### Scope

| Field | Description |
|---|---|

| **Scoping Pattern** | Displays the scoping pattern for the outcome type |

## Steps That Produce This Outcome

- Any step that creates volume zones; added manually via **Insert > Volume Zone Scope**
