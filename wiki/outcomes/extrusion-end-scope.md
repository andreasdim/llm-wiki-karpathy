Outcome that allows scoping the end face created after extrusion, making it available for subsequent operations.

## Overview

**Extrusion End Scope** lets you scope the end face created after an extrusion operation. Add it by right-clicking a **Step** and selecting **Insert > Extrusion End Scope**.

## Steps That Produce This Outcome

- Extrusion operations; added manually via **Insert > Extrusion End Scope**
