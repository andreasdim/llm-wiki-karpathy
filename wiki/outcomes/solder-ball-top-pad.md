Outcome that allows scoping the solder ball top pad created by an operation.

## Overview

**Solder Ball Top Pad** lets you scope the top pad of a solder ball created during an operation.

| Field | Description |
|---|---|

| **Solder Ball Top Pad Scoping Pattern** | Displays the scoping pattern for the Solder Ball Top Pad |

## Steps That Produce This Outcome

- Solder ball creation operations
