Automatically improves topology using predefined repair options in a fixed order.

## Controls

- **Auto Repair** — comprehensive repair with configurable tolerances for edge repair, edge suppression, vertex deletion, partial defeature, thin face removal, short edge collapse, sharp angle removal, face pinching
- **Topology Protection** — protect selected entities from defeaturing
