Repairs topology issues through multiple specialized repair strategies.

## Controls (12 options)

- **Edges of Faces Repair** — repair tolerance
- **Interior Edge Suppression** — suppress interior edges
- **Interior Vertex Deletion** — suppress interior vertices by angle
- **Face Merging** — merge faces by feature angle
- **Edge Suppression** — suppress edges
- **Partial Defeature** — selective geometry merging
- **Thin Face Removal** — remove thin faces
- **Short Edge Collapse** — collapse short edges
- **Sharp Angle Face Removal** — remove sharp-angled faces
- **Face Pinching** — remove small features
- **Hole Closing** — auto-detect and close holes
- **Topology Protection** — prevent defeaturing of selected entities
