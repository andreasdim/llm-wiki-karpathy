Creates volumetric mesh by extruding a meshed 2D base face with divisions along defined edges.

## Controls

- **Stacker: Volume Meshing** — stacking defeature size, default offset size, conformal mesh option, seed/base face scoping
- **Number of Divisions on Edges** — divisions along stacking direction

## Outcomes

- **Scope** — labels for stackable bodies
