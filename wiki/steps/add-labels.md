Creates labels for nodes, edges, faces, and volumes based on scoped entities or proximity radius.

## Controls

- **Proximity Label Addition** — create labels within a proximity radius of a coordinate
- **Scope Label Addition** — create labels from scoped parts, zones, or labels

## Outcomes

- **Scope** — labels for created entities
