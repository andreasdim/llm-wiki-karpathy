Creates patching surfaces for regions identified by Material Points and Hole Patching controls when closed edge loops are unavailable.

## Controls

- **Hole Patching** — element size, part name, face zone name, max hole size
- **Material Point** — define include region (default "Include")
- **Checkpoint** — optional revert capability
