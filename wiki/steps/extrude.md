Generates prismatic element layers from input surface to specified height, creating hexahedral or triangular prism elements.

## Controls

- **Extrusion** — per-layer height, number of layers, growth rate, projection and merge options
- **Scaling Extrusion** — extrude by scaling from a coordinate
- **Custom Names** — specify entity names

## Outcomes

- **Extrusion Start Scope**, **Extrusion End Scope**, **Face Zone Scope**, **Volume Zone Scope**
