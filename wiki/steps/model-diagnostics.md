Diagnoses topology and surface mesh issues to identify problematic features.

## Controls

- **Topology Diagnostics** — detect topology issues (thin stripes, small edges, overlaps, intersections)
- **Topology Diagnostics User Name** — custom label names for issues
- **Surface Mesh Diagnostics** — detect surface mesh issues (self-intersections, free/multi edges)
- **Surface Mesh Diagnostics User Name** — custom label names

## Outcomes

- **Topology Diagnostics**, **Surface Mesh Diagnostics**

## Notes

Automatically added by Mesh Surface or Mesh Volume on failure.
