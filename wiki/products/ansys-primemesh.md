Ansys PrimeMesh is the meshing technology engine that powers Mesh Workflows in Ansys Mechanical.

## Role

PrimeMesh handles all meshing operations within a Mesh Workflow: wrapping, surface meshing, volume meshing, topology operations, morphing, and more. It operates on its own computational domain with Parts, Zones, and Labels.

## Capabilities

- Wrap, pull, extrude meshing procedures
- Complex geometry handling (disconnected, gapped, leaky)
- Built-in execution logic for sequential operations
- Multiple meshing strategies (constant size, size field, MultiZone)
- Topology creation, repair, and diagnostics
