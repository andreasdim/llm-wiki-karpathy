# Manage Zone Properties

The **Manage Zone Properties** operation allows you to assign the physics properties like material properties and thickness on the scoped parts or zones. On completing the Workflow, the defined properties
on the scoped zones or parts are assigned to the geometry parts or bodies.

> Note: **Assign Physics Properties** step is not a mandatory step for completing the mesh workflow.
You can also assign properties to the newly created geometry after completing the workflow.

The **Manage Zone Properties**  operation has the following controls:

* **[Zone Material Assignment](../controls/assign_zone_material.md)**: Defines the material assigned to zones.
* **[Zone Thickness Assignment](../controls/assign_zone_thickness.md)**: Defines the thickness assigned to zones.
