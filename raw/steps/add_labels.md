### Add Labels

The **Add Labels** operation creates labels for the node, edge, face and volume based on the scoped entities or proximity radius.

The **Add Labels** operation has the following controls:

* [**Proximity Label Addition**](../controls/proximity_label_addition.md): Creates labels for edges, faces, volumes, and nodes within a specified location and proximity radius.

* [**Scope Label Addition**](../controls/scope_label_addition.md): Creates labels for edges, faces, volumes, and nodes based on the scoped entities.

The **Add Labels** operation has the following outcome:

* **[Scope](../outcomes/scope.md)**: Scopes the created labels of the **Add Labels** operation.
