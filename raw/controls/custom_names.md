# Custom Names

**Custom Names** create enclosures or extrusions with the provided name.

**Custom Names Details** view has the following options:

**General**

* **[Control Type](../controls.md)**

**Definition**

* **Internal Label Name**: Allows you to provide the name of the label attached to internal entities.
You can click ![forward](../resources/publish.png) on the right corner of 
the option and click **Publish** to publish **Internal Label Name** to the **Property Worksheet**.

* **External Label Name**: Allows you to provide the name of the label attached to external entities.
You can click ![forward](../resources/publish.png) on the right corner of 
the option and click **Publish** to publish **External Label Name** to the **Property Worksheet**.

* **Volume Zone Name**: Allows you to name the volume zone.
You can click ![forward](../resources/publish.png) on the right corner of 
the option and click **Publish** to publish **Volume Zone Name** to the **Property Worksheet**.

* **Volume Label Name**: Allows you to provide name for the volume.
You can click ![forward](../resources/publish.png) on the right corner of 
the option and click **Publish** to publish **Volume Label Name** to the **Property Worksheet**.

* **Face Zone Name**: Allows you to name the face zone.
You can click ![forward](../resources/publish.png) on the right corner of 
the option and click **Publish** to publish **Face Zone Name** to the **Property Worksheet**.
