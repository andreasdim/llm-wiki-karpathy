# Volume Zone Scope
**Volume Zone Scope** allows you to scope the created volume zones in the selected operation.\
Right-click the **Step** and click **Insert > Volume Zone Scope** to add volume zones as an outcome and make it available for scoping in the subsequent operations.

**Volume Zone Scope Details** view has the following options:

**General**
* **[Outcome Type](../outcomes/scope.md)**

**Scope**

* **[Scoping Method](../outcomes/scope.md)**
* **[Scoping Pattern](../outcomes/scope.md)**
