# Quality Metrics

**Quality Metrics** provide information about the quality metrics for the scoped entities in the respective mesh workflow step.
Right-click the **Step** and click **Insert > Quality Metrics** to add **Quality Metrics** as an outcome.

**Quality Metrics Details** view has the following options:

**General**

* **Outcome Type**: Displays the selected outcome type.

**Definition**

* **Max Skewness**: Displays the maximum skewness of the respective mesh workflow step.
* **Min Skewness**: Displays the minimum skewness of the respective mesh workflow step.
* **Max Aspect Ratio**: Displays the maximum aspect ratio of the respective mesh workflow step.
* **Min Aspect Ratio**: Displays the minimum aspect ratio of the respective mesh workflow step.
