# Internal Enclosure Scope
**Internal Enclosure Scope** allows to scope the internal enclosure created in the selected operation.\
Right-click the **Step** and click **Insert > Internal Enclosure Scope** to add internal enclosure as an outcome and make it available for scoping in the subsequent operations.

**Internal Enclosure Scope** Details view has the following options:

**General**
* **[Outcome Type](../outcomes/scope.md)**

**Scope**

* **[Scoping Method](../outcomes/scope.md)**
* **[Scoping Pattern](../outcomes/scope.md)**
