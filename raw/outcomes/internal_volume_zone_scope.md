# Internal Volume Zone Scope

**Internal Volume Zone Scope** allows you to scope the internal volume zones created in the selected operation.\
Right-click the **Step** and click **Insert >  Internal Volume Zone Scope** to add internal volume zone as an outcome and make it available for scoping in subsequent operations.

**Internal Volume Zone Scope** Details view has the following options:

**General**
* **[Outcome Type](../outcomes/scope.md)**

**Scope**

* **[Scoping Method](../outcomes/scope.md)**
* **[Scoping Pattern](../outcomes/scope.md)**
