# Understanding the States of Mesh Workflow Steps

**Mesh Workflow Steps** use the following icons to denote step state:

A check mark ![checkmark](../resources/checkmark.png) denotes a executed step.

A check mark with a hash ![hash](../resources/mshwfhash.png)  denotes a step that is reversible after executed.

A question mark ![mshwfqutmark](../resources/mshwfqutmrk.png)  denotes a step that is blocked that needs input from previous step’s outcome or user-defined values.

A lightning bolt ![thunderbolt](../resources/mshwflightnbolt.png)  indicates that the step is ready to be executed.

A red exclamation mark ![exclamation](../resources/mshwfexclamation.png) denotes that the operation is blocked due to an error during execution.
